What we do

Everything around the care — handled

You bring the diagnosis. We bring the specialists, the plan, the logistics and the people who stay with you the whole way.

Specialist matching

We read your case and match it to specialists who treat your exact condition routinely — not a generalist, the right expert.

Written second opinions

An independent, documented opinion on diagnosis and options — usually within 48 hours — so you can decide with confidence.

Treatment & surgery packages

Bundled, written estimates for procedures, hospital stay and consultant fees — no surprises after you arrive.

Travel & visa logistics

Medical-visa invitation letters, flights, airport pickup and accommodation near the hospital, arranged for you.

Translation & local support

Language help, a local contact at the hospital, and a care manager reachable on WhatsApp throughout your stay.

Aftercare & follow-up

Discharge summaries shared with your home doctor, medication plans, and follow-up reviews coordinated back in Africa.

Four simple steps

1

Send the case

Share de-identified reports by secure WhatsApp or email — diagnosis, imaging and history.

4

Back to you

Your patient returns with discharge summaries and a follow-up plan addressed to you.

Our story

Born in Lagos. Built for Africa.

ASR began with a simple, painful truth: too many families face a serious diagnosis with no idea where to turn. We exist to change that.

Add a photo — your team, your office, or a patient moment

Why we exist

When the care you need isn't down the road, the hardest part isn't the treatment — it's the not knowing. Which specialist? Which hospital? What will it cost? Is travel even worth it?

ASR was founded in Nigeria to answer those questions honestly. We sit on the patient's side of the table: matching each case to a genuine specialist, negotiating clear costs, and walking with families from the first scan to the follow-up back home.

We're proudly West African and pan-African in reach — because everyone, everywhere on the continent, deserves a fair shot at the best care.

What we stand for

Four values we won't compromise

Trust before profit

Accredited hospitals only, and advice that's never steered by commissions. If travel isn't worth it, we'll say so.

Radical transparency

Written estimates up front, in plain language, with no hidden mark-ups or surprise bills.

Dignity & warmth

You are a person, not a case number. We speak your language and move at the pace you need.

Continuity of care

We keep your home doctor in the loop and make sure you return to safe, coordinated follow-up.

Usage

Keep it warm, keep it clear

Do

  • Give the logo plenty of clear space.
  • Lead with red; use yellow and the secondaries as accents.
  • Use the reversed lockup on dark or photo backgrounds.
  • Pair bold, warm, human photography with a clean light base.

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate or recolour the mark.
  • Place the red logo on a busy or low-contrast photo.
  • Swap the fonts for generic system fonts.
  • Crowd one layout with all six colours at once.
